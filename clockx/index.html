What ClockX does

One app for the whole working day.

Built for people who track hours, drive to sites and need clean reports at the end of the month.

01
One-Tap Time Tracking
Check in when you start, check out when you're done. A live timer keeps count, and every entry lands in your daily total — with start, end and project.
02
Projects
Create colour-coded projects — Office, Site North, Client Harper — and start or pause tracking per project. Always see what's running at a glance.
03
To-Dos
A simple task list right on your home screen. Add tasks in seconds, star the important ones and tick them off as the day moves on.
04
Trips & Mileage Log
Log business trips with route, date, licence plate, purpose and distance. Repeat a frequent trip with one tap and keep a running kilometre total.
05
Analysis & Export
See your hours per project and trip totals by month, year or any free date range — then export as CSV or PDF, or share the report directly.
06
Shift & Vacation Calendar
Plan shifts, mark vacation days and keep an eye on your yearly vacation count. Sync with your iPhone calendar if you want to.
Your data, your choice

Local on your device

Keep everything offline on your iPhone. No account, no server — all data stays on this device, always.

Option 2

Cloud saving

Prefer a backup? Turn on cloud saving and your times, trips and to-dos are stored securely and stay with you across devices.
