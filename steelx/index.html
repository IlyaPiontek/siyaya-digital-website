What Steel X does

Professional tools, built for the yard.

Every feature designed for how steel professionals actually work.

01
AI Tube Counting
Point your camera at a tube bundle end-face. Steel X counts circular cross-sections automatically with a confidence percentage and manual correction.
02
OD / ID / WT Measurement
An on-screen physical ruler with draggable lines to measure outer diameter, inner diameter and wall thickness directly against the tube.
03
Inventory Sessions
Create named inventory sessions with line items. Log product number, grade, OD×WT, length, quantity and heat number per item.
04
Label OCR Scanner
Scan tube labels with your camera. Steel X reads product number, grade, dimensions, length, quantity and heat number automatically.
05
Weight Calculator
Automatic weight calculation per line item and grand total. Uses the standard steel tube formula with selectable material density.
06
Excel Export
Export any session to a clean .xlsx file with all columns, weight totals and photo indicators. Share via email, AirDrop or any iOS share target.
