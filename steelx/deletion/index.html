Data Deletion

Steel X · Siyaya Digital · Last updated: April 2026

Steel X stores all data exclusively on your device. We never collect, transmit or store your data on any server. You have full control over your data at all times.

Option 1 — Clear Data in the App

Delete all sessions, items and statistics without uninstalling:

1
Open Steel X and tap the Settings tab
2
Scroll down to the Data section
3
Tap Clear All Data
4
Confirm by tapping Delete Everything

This permanently deletes all sessions, inventory items and resets your export count. This action cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Delete Individual Sessions

Go to the Sessions tab, long-press on any session and tap Delete. All items within that session will be permanently removed.

Option 3 — Uninstall the App

Deleting Steel X from your device permanently removes all app data. Long-press the Steel X icon on your home screen and tap "Remove App" → "Delete App".

What Data Exists

Steel X stores the following locally: inventory sessions (names, dates), tube items (product numbers, grades, dimensions, heat numbers, quantities, notes), measurement unit preference (mm or inches), and an export counter. No personal information, account data or usage analytics are stored.

No Server-Side Data

Since Steel X does not transmit any data to our servers, there is no server-side data to delete. The deletion methods above are complete — no further action is needed on our end.
