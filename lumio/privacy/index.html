Privacy Policy

Lumio — Flashlight  ·  Effective: April 2026  ·  siyaya-digital.com/lumio/privacy
🔒
The short version: We collect absolutely nothing.
Lumio is a fully local app. It does not connect to any server, collect any personal data, or require an account. Nothing is ever sent to us — because there is nowhere to send it.
§ 1
Who We Are

Lumio is developed and published by Siyaya Digital, operated by Ilya Piontek (IP Strategy UG), Niederrheinstraße 46f, 41472 Neuss, Germany.

Contact: legal@siyaya-digital.com

§ 2
Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Lumio is a fully local app. It does not:

  • Require an account or registration
  • Connect to any server or the internet
  • Collect your name, email, location, or device identifiers
  • Use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs
  • Display advertisements
  • Share any data with third parties
§ 3
Device Permissions

Lumio requests only one permission: Camera / Torch (LED Flash) — required solely to control the device's built-in flashlight LED. No camera images are ever captured, stored, or transmitted.

§ 4
App Settings & Preferences

Any preferences you configure in Lumio (such as "Torch on at Launch" or "Keep Screen Awake") are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

§ 5
Apple & App Store

When you download Lumio from the App Store, Apple may collect certain data as part of their standard App Store operations (such as purchase records). This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours. We have no access to this data.

§ 6
Children's Privacy

Lumio does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

§ 7
GDPR (EU Users)

Because we collect no personal data, Lumio is not subject to most GDPR obligations. We are not a data controller under GDPR. No data processing agreement is required.

§ 8
Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this policy and notify users through App Store update notes. The current version is always available at: siyaya-digital.com/lumio/privacy
