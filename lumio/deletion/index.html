Data Deletion

Required by Apple App Store guidelines
✅
No server-side data to delete.
Because Lumio collects no personal data and operates entirely offline, there is no data stored on our servers. Everything is on your device only.
What Lumio stores
Local preferences only

Lumio stores only your in-app preferences locally on your iPhone, such as:

  • Whether "Torch on at Launch" is enabled
  • Whether "Keep Screen Awake" is enabled
  • Your last selected strobe frequency

This data is stored using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults) and never leaves your device.

How to delete
Remove all app data

To completely remove all data associated with Lumio:

  • On your iPhone, go to Settings
  • Scroll down and tap General → iPhone Storage
  • Find Lumio in the list
  • Tap Delete App and confirm

This permanently deletes the app and all associated local data from your device.

Alternatively, reset app preferences without uninstalling: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Lumio → Offload App.
